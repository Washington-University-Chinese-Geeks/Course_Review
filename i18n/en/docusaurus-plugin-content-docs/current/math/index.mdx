# Mathematics & Statistics (MATH)

Overview of undergraduate and graduate courses from WashU's Department of Mathematics and Statistics. No student reviews yet — open a PR to add the first one!

> **Course-number change**: WashU recently migrated 3-digit course numbers to 4-digit ones (e.g., MATH 131 → MATH 1310, MATH 310 → MATH 3010, MATH 429 → MATH 4301, MATH 430 → MATH 4302). Old numbers are listed on each course card when known.
